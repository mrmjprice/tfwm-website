Network Development Plans

We want to provide modern transport services that meet people’s needs. We write network development plans to help do this.

We review these plans every year to make sure they are still relevant as the region grows and changes.

We’re writing plans for 7 areas of the West Midlands. You can read network development plans for:

  • Dudley
  • West and South Birmingham

We’ll soon publish plans for:

  • Coventry
  • North Birmingham
  • Sandwell
  • Solihull
  • Walsall and Wolverhampton
